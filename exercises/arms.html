Arm Strength

As with all exercises, it's incredibly important to properly stretch and stay hydrated.

  • Bicep Curls: This popular exercise has been known to tone and strengthen arm muscles.
  • Lateral Raises: By lifting weights up and away from your body, you strenthen your whole arms and shoulders.
  • Shoulder Press: Lifting a weight above your head, similarly to lateral raises, helps strengthen the full arm.
