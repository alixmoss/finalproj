Ankle and Foot Strength and Stability

As with all exercises, it's incredibly important to properly stretch and stay hydrated.

  • Ankle Alphabet: Simple but effective, spelling out the alphabet with your toes can help increase ankle stability.
  • Calf Raises: This exercise helps improve strength from your calf to balls of your feet.
  • Single-Leg Balance: By balancing on one leg, you help increase your strength and dependability in your ankles, feet, and legs.
