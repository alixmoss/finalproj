Core Strength and Posture

As with all exercises, it's incredibly important to properly stretch and stay hydrated.

  • Plank: Holding a plank for as long as you can helps your entire torso.
  • Bird-Dog Exercises: On your hands and knees, extend one arm and one leg away from your torso.
  • Crunches: A classic exercise, crunches and sit-ups are well-known to improve core muscles.
