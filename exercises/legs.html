Leg Strength

As with all exercises, it's incredibly important to properly stretch and stay hydrated.

  • Lunges: Despite the variation you may use, lunges can help strengthen your thighs and calves alike.
  • Squats: Whether doing regular squats or jumping squats, this exercise is sure to make your thighs burn.
  • Leg lifts: This simple exercise has many kinds to suit your skill level.
