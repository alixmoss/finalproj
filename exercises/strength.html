Strength

As with all exercises, it's incredibly important to properly stretch and stay hydrated.

  • Weightlifting: Lifting weights can help you target a specific area of your body that needs more attention. Make sure to engage your core!
  • Resistance bands: Working with resistance bands can help strengthen weakened muscles
  • Presses: Whether it's bench presses or leg presses, pushing weight is much more likely to increase your strength.
