Neck Strength and Posture

As with all exercises, it's incredibly important to properly stretch and stay hydrated.

  • Chin tucks: By pulling your chin towards your neck and holding for a few seconds, you can improve your posture and increase strength in your neck muscles.
  • Neck Flexion/Extension: Relax your neck muscles until you look straight up or straight down for a few seconds before pulling back to your normal position.
  • Isometric neck strengthening: Place your palm on your forehead, and resist flexion of your neck while keeping your head still; hold, and then relax.
